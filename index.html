Travel & Scenery

Japan offers breathtaking landscapes across four distinct seasons ‚Äî from snow-capped mountains to tropical beaches, ancient temples to neon-lit cities. Discover the beauty that awaits!

Must-Visit Destinations

  • Mount Fuji (ÂØåÂ£´Â±±) ‚Äî Japan's iconic sacred mountain

Seasonal Highlights

Spring brings cherry blossoms, summer offers vibrant festivals and fireworks, autumn paints the landscape in red and gold foliage, and winter transforms the north into a snowy paradise. Each season reveals a different face of Japan.

Travel Tips & Etiquette

Remember to bow when greeting, remove shoes before entering homes and some restaurants, and avoid eating while walking. The Japan Rail Pass is essential for travelers, and convenience stores (konbini) are your best friend!
